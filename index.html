Frequently asked questions!

  • What is Dentist Channel.Online?

    DentistChannel.Online is a digital platform offering educational resources, webinars, and on-demand videos for dental students and professionals.

  • Who can use Dentist Channel.Online?

    Dentists, dental students, and professionals in the oral healthcare field can access the platform for learning and networking.

  • What is Dentist Channel Online Prime Membership?

    Dentist Channel Online Prime Membership allows members to attend live and recorded webinars and receive a participation certificate affiliated with the LZK Rheinland Pfalz (Germany) for Continuing Dental Education (CDE) points. Membership is valid for one year and offers a wide range of benefits for dental professionals.

  • How many webinars are conducted monthly?

    Dentist Channel Online typically conducts 5 to 7 webinars per month, covering various dental topics and specialties. The schedule may vary based on demand and speaker availability.

  • What happens if I miss attending a webinar?

    If you miss a webinar, don't worry! Most webinars are recorded and made available in our Premium Videos section. You can log in to your account and watch the recording at your convenience.

  • Who can join webinars and watch premium videos?

    Webinars and premium videos are exclusively accessible to Prime Members of Dentist Channel Online. Becoming a Prime Member unlocks access to expert-led webinars, premium content, and other exclusive resources.

  • Will I receive a certificate for attending webinars?

    Yes, you will receive a certificate for every webinar you attend. Additionally, Dentist Channel Online webinars are accredited by LZK Rheinland Pfalz (Germany), allowing you to earn CDE (Continuing Dental Education) points for each event.

  • What is the average duration of a webinar?

    The average duration of a webinar on Dentist Channel Online is between 45 to 60 minutes. This allows for an in-depth discussion of the topic, followed by a Q&A session to address attendee questions.

  • How will I be notified about upcoming webinars?

    You can stay updated about upcoming webinars by joining our WhatsApp community. Simply contact our office to be added, and you'll receive regular updates. Additionally, you can visit our website to register for any upcoming webinars directly.

  • How old is Dentist Channel Online?

    Dentist Channel Online is 6 years old. It is registered as a private limited company under CIN U74999MH2019PTC322775 and holds GST 27AAHCD1511B1ZC.

  • Can I be a speaker for Dentist Channel Online webinars?

    Yes, you can! If you're interested in becoming a speaker for our webinars, please reach out to us at office@dentistchannel.online. Our team will review your inquiry and get back to you with further details. We welcome dental professionals with expertise to share their knowledge with our community!

  • Why do I need to take a membership?

    Taking a membership gives you access to 5-7 webinars per month, as well as the ability to watch recorded webinars anytime. You'll also earn CE points for every event you attend. Your membership helps us bring more innovative content and resources to you. The cost is just the equivalent of 2-3 standard coffees, but the value you gain in professional development is priceless!
